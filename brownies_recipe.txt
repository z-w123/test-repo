World's Best Fudgiest Brownies!

https://cafedelites.com/worlds-best-fudgiest-brownies/

INGREDIENTS
1 cup 8oz/240g vegan butter (made from plant-based milk), melted and cooled
2 tablespoons (30ml) vegetable oil
1 1/4 cups (9oz/260g) date sugar
1 cup (7oz/200g) coconut sugar
4 ripe bananas, mashed
1 tablespoon (15ml) pure vanilla extract
3/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup (3.5oz/130g) all purpose flour
1 cup (3.5oz/100g) good quality, unsweetened cocoa powder
7 oz (200g) roughly vegan chopped chocolate or large chocolate chips
7 oz (200g) walnuts

instructions only!
